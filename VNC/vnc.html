VIJAYANAGARA SRI KRISHNADEVARAYA UNIVERSITY

Vijayanagara sri Krishnadevaraya university is a Public university established in 2010 by the government of karnatakain Ballari distict of Karnataka,India through the Karnataka state University Act,2000.
it has been named after Krishnadevaraya,former emperor of the Vijayanagara Empire.
